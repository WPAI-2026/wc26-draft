RULES How It Works

Snake order. Round 1 runs Rob → Mike → Freddie → Ryan → Jordan → Chris → Will → Dean. Round 2 flips it (Dean first), Round 3 flips back, and so on for 6 rounds.
Everyone gets 6 nations. 8 drafters × 6 rounds = all 48 World Cup teams drafted. Nothing left on the shelf.
No time limit. Take all the time you want — the board waits. It only unlocks the pick button for whoever is on the clock.
Live for everyone. When someone drafts, it updates on every device within a few seconds. Keep the page open to watch it unfold.
Winning. However you've agreed to score it — last team standing, furthest run, total goals. The draft just hands out the nations; the glory's on you.
